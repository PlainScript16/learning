Send Splendid Flowers to Delhi- A Place with Amazing Gardens

The capital city of India, New Delhi is a perfect example of old world charm (monuments) with a modern outlook through rapid urbanization, expanding metro infrastructure, high rise buildings and various posh localities. Inspite of embracing development with open warms, the city of Delhi has still vast stretches of green gardens, forest covers and a sprawling greenery across various acres. The historical city is also known for its magnificent gardens. Some of the eye appealing and vibrant gardens of Delhi are Garden of five senses, Kamla Nehru Ridge, Lodhi Garden, Deer Park, Sanjay Lake Park, Kalindi Kunj Park, Millenium Indraprastha Park and a lot of other impressive gardens. These lovely gardens are adorned with beautiful flowers and can easily captivate the attention of any visitor.
